About me

I am a recent graduate of the University of Virginia, where I studied English and Computer Science. I have worked as a Software Engineer at Brightspot for just over a year.

The work featured on this website focuses primarily on applying digital modes of analysis to literature. An area I keep coming back to is the effect of technology on how we use and understand language. My posts and projects reflect my best efforts to complicate and understand this phenomenon.

The Blog on this website is fairly informal; jump over to the Projects vertical to take a look at the more ambitious endeavors I've worked on over the past year. At work I use Java and MySQL, but most of the code featured in those projects is written in R or Python.
